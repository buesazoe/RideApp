Welcome to our cutting-edge booking app designed to streamline and enhance your motorcycle-related experiences!

Our application is built on a robust foundation, encapsulated in the models.py file, which meticulously defines the key entities that power the system.

Core Entities:

  • Motorcycle Brand: Represents the distinct brands of motorcycles available. Ensures uniqueness with a name attribute.
  • Motorcycle Model: Tightly linked to Motorcycle Brand through a foreign key relationship. Captures essential details such as name, production year, and brand affiliation.
  • Customer: Integrates seamlessly with the Django authentication system. One-to-One relationship with the User model for a personalized experience. Collects additional contact information like phone number and email.
  • Booking: Facilitates the reservation process by associating customers with specific motorcycle models. Records essential booking details such as date, start time, and end time.
  • Service: Encompasses a range of services available for motorcycles. Provides a comprehensive description and pricing information.

Special Features:

  • User Authentication: Leverages the Django User model for secure and user-friendly authentication.
  • Temporal Tracking: Employs created_at and updated_at timestamps to monitor the lifecycle of entities.

How It Works:

Our booking app simplifies the motorcycle booking process. Customers can seamlessly browse through various motorcycle brands and models, selecting the desired one for their adventure. With integrated user authentication, customers can enjoy a personalized experience, managing bookings effortlessly. The temporal tracking ensures up-to-date information, enhancing transparency throughout the system.

Whether you are a motorcycle enthusiast seeking a thrilling ride or a service provider offering top-notch maintenance, our booking app caters to your needs.
